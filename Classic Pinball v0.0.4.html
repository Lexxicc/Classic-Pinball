How to Play

OBJECTIVE

Keep the ball alive and rack up the highest score you can before losing all 5 balls. Every shot counts — the longer your ball stays up, the more every hit is worth.

CONTROLS
LEFT PADDLE — Tap left half of screen / Arrow Left / Z
RIGHT PADDLE — Tap right half of screen / Arrow Right / /

Press the left or right paddle to launch — left fires up-right, right fires up-left.

BUMPERS
• First hit on any bumper — 100 pts
• Repeat hits on the same bumper — 50 pts
• Hit all bumpers in one ball — 1,000 pt bonus
• Top lane shot — 750 pts (once per ball)
RALLY MULTIPLIER

The longer your ball stays alive, the higher the multiplier. It starts at x1.0 and rises by +0.25 every ~10 seconds, up to a max of x3.0. Every point scored is multiplied by the current value. Lose the ball and it resets to x1.0.
